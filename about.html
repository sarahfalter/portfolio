Who am I?

My name is Sarah, I'm a graduate student at the University of Florida working towards my Master of Arts in Mass Communications with a focus in Web Design and Online Communications. I'm an artist, and spend a lot of my free time painting. I'm also an avid cat lover, coffee enthusiast, and love traveling.

My Background

I received my Bachelor's in Psychology in 2018 from Florida Atlantic University. My years of psychology studies prepared me well to understand consumer behavior, and my affinity for art allows me to create materials that evoke meaningful emotions. Now I'm learning how to code so that I can bring my artistic side to the tech world. If I seem like a good fit for your project, send me a message below!
